# capitalcitysoftware.com

## What It Is

A local SEO lead generation property for [[20_areas/big-city-software|Big City Software]]. The domain name plays on "capital city" as a regional identifier (e.g. Tallahassee is Florida's capital). Paired with [[10_projects/firstcitysoftware.com|firstcitysoftware.com]], which targets "first cities."

## Strategy

Same approach as firstcitysoftware.com: locale-specific landing pages per state, targeting capital cities (e.g. `capitalcitysoftware.com/tallahassee`, `/montgomery`, etc.). All pages sell Big City Software products and services — Big City Software branding throughout, no separate identity.

- Locale pages are the primary SEO target
- Generic fallback landing page at root for unrouted traffic
- Geo-detect or explicit routing to push root visitors to the right locale page

## Status

Domain owned. Nothing built yet. **On hold — not worth building until Big City Software has products/services to sell through it.**

## When to Build

- Big City Software is generating revenue or has a clear launch-ready product
- Revenue from freelancing has stabilized
- Build in tandem with firstcitysoftware.com rebuild
