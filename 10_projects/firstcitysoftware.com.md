# firstcitysoftware.com

## What It Is

A local SEO lead generation property for [[20_areas/big-city-software|Big City Software]]. The domain name plays on "first city" as a regional identifier (e.g. Pensacola is Florida's "first city"). Paired with [[10_projects/capitalcitysoftware.com|capitalcitysoftware.com]], which targets capital cities.

## Strategy

Multi-locale landing page approach: each state gets locale-specific pages targeting its "first city" (e.g. `/pensacola`, `/mobile`, etc.). All pages sell Big City Software products and services — no separate identity, Big City Software branding throughout.

- Locale pages are the primary SEO target (e.g. `firstcitysoftware.com/pensacola`)
- A generic fallback landing page exists at the root for direct/unrouted traffic
- Users landing on the root should be redirected to the most relevant locale page where possible (geo-detect or explicit routing)

## Status

Currently live as a low-quality static site. Needs a full rebuild before it's worth anything. **On hold — not worth building until Big City Software has products/services to sell through it.**

## When to Build

- Big City Software is generating revenue or has a clear launch-ready product
- Revenue from freelancing has stabilized
- Not before then
